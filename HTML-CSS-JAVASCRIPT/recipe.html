How to make tea

A warming spiced tea, that's the perfect match for our coconut chai traybake. A quick and easy brew that can be made with almond or cow's milk.

Ingredients

  • Tea bag
  • Water
  • Milk - Dairy/Plant-based (Optional)
  • Sugar/Honey

Preparation

  1. Heat the milk in a saucepan over a very low heat.
  2. Empty the contents of the tea bags into the pan, then add the cracked cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, nutmeg and cloves.
  3. Sweeten with light brown soft sugar to taste (chai tea should be sweet, but use less if you like).
  4. Then leave to infuse for 10 minutes
  5. Strain into mugs and enjoy.
